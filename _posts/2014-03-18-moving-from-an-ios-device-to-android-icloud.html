Admittedly moving from an iOS device to an android device can seem a bit daunting, especially if you use iCloud for all your syncing. Thankfully there are a few apps on the Google play store that help connect your droid to icloud!

Note: Please be sure to read reviews before download the apps from the store. These have been found while doing research for a new smart phone purchase. I do not have a droid to test on currently.

SmoothSync for Cloud Calendar

Syncs: calendars and reminders
Store Link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.dmfs.caldav.icloud
Description from Store: SmoothSync for Cloud Calendar syncs your iCloud calendars and reminders. Due to its implementation as sync adapter it integrates seamlessly with the native calendar app and widgets and operates invisible in the background.

Reminder For iCloud/iOS/iPhone

Syncs: Reminders
Store Link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=us.doplus.reminder
Description from Store: This is a sole Android to-do reminder app that sync with Reminders of iCloud (iOS/Mac). All you have to do is creating a reminder on your phone and it will reminder you at the right time so that you won't miss any important task. It is designed to sync with the Reminder of iCloud. So, an iCloud account is needed before using this application.

iNotes - Sync Note with iOS

Syncs: notes
Store Link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.dayup.inotes
Description from Store: iNotes is an easy notes app for synchronizing your notes of Android to Notes app on Mac/iOS(iphone, ipad) . It is currently applicable to iCloud, Gmail,Yahoo, Aol,163 only.

SmoothSync for Cloud Contacts

Syncs: Contacts
Store Link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.dmfs.carddav.icloud
Description from Store: SmoothSync for Cloud Contacts syncs your iCloud contacts. Due to its implementation as sync adapter it integrates seamlessly with the native contacts app and operates invisible in the background.

iCloud Mail Sync

App: Blue Mail - Email & Mailboxes
Store Link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.trtf.blue
Description from Store: Blue Mail is a free and universal email client allowing you to use and manage as many email accounts as you desire at the same time to create a powerful Unified Inbox experience with an extraordinary and modern design. Blue Mail supports Gmail, Yahoo, Office 365, AOL, Google Apps, Hotmail, Outlook, iCloud, Mail.ru, Zoho, GMX and any other IMAP mailbox!

Alternative Option - Imap settings for icloud: https://discussions.apple.com/thread/3636567?start=15&tstart=0

iCloud HD

Syncs: seems to be a portal for icloud site (on tablets only)
Store Link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.datheuma.icloud
Description from Store: We present to you iCloud on Android. Access the iCloud control panel from the comfort of your Android tablet. Use your iCloud login and password to log in and you are ready to go.
